CALDERA

Cyber Adversary Language and Decision Engine for Red Team Automation

CALDERA is a framework designed to quickly run breach-and-simulation exercises. It was originally developed as a MITRE research & development program, aimed at automating the decision-making of a cyber adversary. Nowadays, it can also be used in realistic red-and-blue exercises. CALDERA is built on top of the ATT&CK framework. Using the plugins, deploy sandcat (54ndc47) agents on target hosts and run operations against them in chain mode.
